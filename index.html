Types of Arts

Art comes in many beautiful forms, each expressing emotion, culture, and creativity.

Art is a universal language. It can be seen, heard, and felt globally.

Visual Arts

Painting, drawing, sculpture, and photography allow artists to capture captured moments.

Performing Arts

Dance, music, and theater bring stories and emotions to life through live performance.

Literary Arts

Poetry, novels, and essays use language to evoke imagination and deep thought.

Digital Arts

Animation, graphic design, and digital illustration blend technology and modern art.
