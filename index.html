Types of Arts

Art comes in many beautiful forms, each expressing emotion, culture, and imagination in a unique way.

Art is a universal language. It can be seen, heard, performed, or even felt through movement, color, and words.

Visual Arts

Painting, drawing, sculpture, and photography allow artists to create images that capture beauty and meaning.

Performing Arts

Dance, music, and theater bring stories and emotions to life through live expression and movement.

Literary Arts

Poetry, novels, and essays use language to evoke imagination, ideas, and deep human experiences.

Digital Arts

Animation, graphic design, and digital illustration blend technology with creativity in modern ways.
